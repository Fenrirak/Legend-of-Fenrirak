About Legend of Fenrirak

Legend Of Fenrirak was orginally created by four year 7s at a New Zealand intermediate school called Murrays Bay Intermediate (Learn more about them in the Meet the Creators section!) and we have been upgrading and expanding Legend Of Fenrirak since then. Currently Legend of Fenrirak has 2 board games, 3 in progress story books, 1 in progress online version and countless other plans that haven't been started yet!
