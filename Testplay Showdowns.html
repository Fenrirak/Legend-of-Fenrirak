Legend Of Fenrirak™
-Showdowns-

Objective

Defeat all other players on the board. If you (the human player) defeat all three computer opponents, you win — even if it's not your turn. If a computer defeats you, the game ends in defeat.

Setting Up

All players choose a class (Mage, Knight, or Archer) and a starting weapon. Players then choose a starting tile. Everyone rolls a die — highest to lowest determines turn order. Ties reroll among tied players.

Turn Structure

Players may move before attacking but cannot move after attacking. Opening a chest ends the turn immediately. One turn ends when a character has opened a chest, attacked, or clicked End Turn.

Movement

Move up to your movement speed in adjacent (non-diagonal) tiles. Difficult Terrain costs 2 tiles per tile (Knights ignore this). Thorny Vines deal 2 damage when passing through and 4 damage if you stop in them, then 3 damage every 2 turn cycles until you leave.

Attacks & Hit Throws

Before attacking, roll a Hit Throw. If the total (including modifiers) equals or exceeds the target's Defence, the attack hits. Range is measured as the greater of horizontal or vertical distance.

Rocks, Logs, Trees, and Chests never block a ranged attack outright - they only grant a defence bonus (see Cover below); they don't stop the shot from passing through.

Cover

  • Partial Cover (PC): +1 Defence if it's directly adjacent (non-diagonally) to the target AND the attacker's line of fire actually passes through it on the way to the target. Cover next to the target that the shot isn't coming through (e.g. an attack from behind) gives no bonus. Mages treat PC as Heavy Cover for this bonus too.
  • Heavy Cover (HC): +2 Defence in the same situation (+3 if the target is a Knight).
  • Rocks and Logs count as Partial Cover. Trees and Chests count as Heavy Cover.

High Ground

Blue tiles are High Ground. Attacker on High Ground gets +1 to Hit Throw.

Chests

Opening a chest ends your turn. You must be directly adjacent (non-diagonal). Roll a die for loot. A chest can only be opened once. If a weapon is received, it goes to inventory automatically.

Healing & Potions

Can't use Potion of Healing at full health. Can't heal above max health. Potions can be used any time during your turn before it ends.

Death

Health reduced to 0 or below = defeated and removed from the game.

Classes

Mage: 65 HP / Defence 3 / Move 4. Passive: All PC becomes HC; Thorny Vines take one extra turn cycle to deal damage.

Knight: 75 HP / Defence 5 / Move 6. Passive: Difficult terrain counts as 1 tile (no movement penalty; damage from Thorny Vines still applies).

Archer: 70 HP / Defence 4 / Move 5. Passive: +1 Hit Throw & +1 Range when target is on lower terrain than the Archer.

Enter Your Name

Maximum 7 characters (letters, numbers, symbols, spaces). At least one visible character.
